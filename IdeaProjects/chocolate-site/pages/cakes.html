  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

  • Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.

2. TRIPPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of toast (or biscuits)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Instruction

  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • Separate the yolks from the whites.
  • Chop the egg whites into small pieces.
  • Crumble the egg yolks and set aside.
  • In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  • Stir in the flour to make a roux, cooking until it is bubbly but not browned.
  • Gradually whisk in the milk, continuing to stir until the sauce thickens and is smooth.
  • Add the chopped egg whites to the sauce.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Toast the bread slices or prepare the biscuits.
  • Spoon the creamy egg mixture over the toast or biscuits.
  • Sprinkle the crumbled egg yolks over the top.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  • Serve immediately while hot.

3. MAGIC CRUST BLENDER CAKE

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Bisquick or any all-purpose baking mix
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your favorite)
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked bacon, ham, or your choice of protein
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instruction

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9-inch pie dish or a similar-sized baking dish.
  • In a blender, combine the eggs, milk, Bisquick, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and dried oregano (if using). Blend on high until smooth and well combined.
  • Add the grated cheese, chopped protein (bacon, ham, etc.), onion, and bell pepper to the blender. Pulse a few times to mix the ingredients without completely blending them.
  • Pour the blended mixture into the prepared pie dish.
  • Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the quiche is golden brown on top and set in the center. A toothpick inserted into the center should come out clean.
  • Allow the quiche to cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Tips

  • Customize your quiche by adding your favorite vegetables, meats, or cheeses.
  • For a vegetarian option, omit the meat and add extra veggies like spinach, mushrooms, or tomatoes.
  • Serve with a side salad or fresh fruit for a complete meal.

4. FRITTATA WITH HAM & ROASTED CAKE

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup cooked ham, diced
  • 1 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instruction

  • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a large ovenproof skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until they are softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  • Add the diced ham and sliced roasted red peppers to the skillet, stirring to combine. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.
  • Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Pour the egg mixture over the ham and roasted peppers in the skillet, spreading it evenly.
  • Cook on the stovetop over medium heat for about 5 minutes, or until the edges start to set but the center is still slightly runny.
  • Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the frittata is puffed and golden brown, and the center is set.
  • Remove the skillet from the oven and let the frittata cool for a few minutes.
  • Slice into wedges and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

Tips

  • For a more vibrant frittata, you can use a mix of different colored bell peppers.
  • Feel free to add other ingredients like spinach, mushrooms, or feta cheese for extra flavor and nutrition.
  • Serve the frittata warm or at room temperature, with a side salad or crusty bread.
